Invited talk

  • The Chronicle of Software Vulnerability Detection (Jun 2023, @ Kyung Hee University) [pdf] [slide]

Abstract

With the threats to cybersecurity ever-increasing, prematurely detecting vulnerabilities in software systems is deemed as one of the promising ways to thwart exploitation attempts from attackers at the fundamental level. In this talk, we first briefly recap software vulnerabilities and move on to the historical evolution of vulnerability detection techniques (i.e., how it has evolved up until now). Finally, we go over the current most popular vulnerability detection technique (i.e., fuzzing) and discuss its possible future development.
