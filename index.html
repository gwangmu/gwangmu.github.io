Gwangmu Lee

Software Engineer @ Swiss National Computing Centre (CSCS).

In academia, I’m mainly interested in dynamic software testing (or “fuzzing”) and Linux kernel security. I also have a research background in compiler optimization and computer architecture, and have studied physics and astrology during my Bachelor’s years. Currently, I work at Swiss National Computer Centre (CSCS) as a software engineer for container engine development.
